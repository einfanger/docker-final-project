The Last of Us Survival Board

Field notes, survival tips, and ways to not get folded by infected.

Survival Tips

  • Stay quiet. Infected hear everything, and humans are usually worse.
  • Always check drawers, shelves, and abandoned buildings for supplies.
  • Use stealth whenever possible to save ammo and avoid drawing attention.
  • Craft medkits and shivs as early as you can.
  • Never assume an area is clear just because it looks empty.

Combat Tricks

  • Brick and bottle stuns are elite — use them before rushing in.
  • Headshots save ammo and end fights faster.
  • Melee weapons are gold, so don’t waste them on weak enemies unless needed.
  • Listen Mode helps, but don’t rely on it like it’s wall hacks from heaven.
  • Move after every loud shot so enemies don’t pin your location.

Loot Priorities

  • Rags and alcohol for medkits and molotovs.
  • Blades and binding for shivs.
  • Explosives for traps and bombs.
  • Ammo only when you really need it — don’t spray and pray.
  • Supplements and parts for upgrades that actually matter.

Important Reminder

  • Clickers are basically nightmare chickens with sonar, so do not get close.
  • Humans flank more than you think, so watch your sides.
  • Running blindly gets you killed. Slow and smart wins.
  • If you can avoid a fight, that is sometimes the best fight.
  • Hope is good. Ammo is better.
